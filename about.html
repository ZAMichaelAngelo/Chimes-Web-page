Our Story

Chimes Crane Hire is a trusted name in South Africa's crane industry, specializing in mobile crane hire, rigging, and heavy lifting. We're committed to delivering safe, efficient solutions with a personal touch.

1990s: Roots in Beacon Crane Hire

Founders Paul Chimes and Elias Nyathi built their expertise running the successful Beacon Crane Hire.

2003: Founding of Chimes

Paul and Elias establish Chimes Crane Hire in Johannesburg, bringing decades of industry knowledge.

2010s: Fleet Expansion & Achievements

Grew to a fleet of Tadano cranes (8-275 tons), achieved BEE Level 5 certification, and expanded services to include crane trucks and trucks.

Today: Leader in Safety & Relations

Owner-managed with 11-50 dedicated professionals, focusing on customer satisfaction and rigorous safety standards.
